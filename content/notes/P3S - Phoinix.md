## Encounter Info

>**DPS Check:** ~42,200 DPS

>**Enrage**: ~11:03 (depends on how fast add phase is completed)

### Drops
**Chest One**
- 1 coffer selected randomly from the following:
	- Head
	- Hand
	- Foot

**Chest Two**
- 1 Leg coffer
- Radiant Twine (upgrade for tome armor)
- Radiant Roborant (upgrade for tome weapon)

**Guaranteed**
- Page
