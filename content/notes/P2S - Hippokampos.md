## Encounter Info

>**DPS Check:** ~41,900 DPS

>**Enrage**: 10:28

### Drops
**Chest One**
- 1 coffer selected randomly from the following:
	- Head
	- Hand
	- Foot
	
**Chest Two**
- 1 coffer selected randomly from the following:
	- Head
	- Hand
	- Foot
- Discal Tomestone
- Radiant Coating (upgrade for tome accessory)

**Guaranteed**
- Page
