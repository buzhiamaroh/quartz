**Chest One**
- 1 coffer selected randomly from the following:
	- Head
	- Hand
	- Foot
	
**Chest Two**
- 1 coffer selected randomly from the following:
	- Head
	- Hand
	- Foot
- Discal Tomestone
- Radiant Coating (upgrade for tome accessory)

- Page
